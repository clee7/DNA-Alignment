This instruction sequence is the alignment for DNA sequences obtained from Influenza A viruses from cat in Russia (s string) and Austria (t string). Note that the alignment score for these two DNA sequences are high with comparatively little mismatch and gap. This result means that despite regional differences, the influenza A viruses in cat are mostly the same. The mismatched part can be the result of mutation while copying DNA sequences (single nucleotide mutation).  Note that gaps occur mostly at the end of the sequence. This result can hint to the possibility that more mutation/improper copying of gene happens at the end of the sequence.
